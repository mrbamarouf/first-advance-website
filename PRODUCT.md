# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Saudi business owners, executives, investors, real estate stakeholders, and operations leaders evaluating First Advance as a trusted partner for business, operational, and real estate services.

## Product Purpose
Present First Advance as a premium Saudi corporate services company. The site should communicate trust, practical capability, integrated service coverage, and a clear path to contact through direct messaging, email, or direct inquiry.

## Brand Personality
Calm, trustworthy, and executive. The experience should feel like a high-end Saudi corporate office: practical, organized, restrained, and ready for live publication.

## Anti-references
Do not redesign the approved website concept, rewrite approved Arabic content, invent new services, change the official logo, use Dubai/Burj Khalifa imagery, use portraits, use AI faces, use businessmen photos, or make the site feel like a generic startup/SaaS landing page.

## Design Principles
1. Preserve approved Arabic content and the official First Advance logo.
2. Use only Saudi architecture, Saudi city, office, boardroom, real estate, business district, and infrastructure imagery.
3. Keep the visual tone premium and calm: forest green, warm white, and gold as a restrained accent.
4. Make mobile reading and navigation feel composed from top to bottom.
5. Ship a production-ready surface: responsive, fast, accessible, and build-clean.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus states, descriptive image alt text, reduced-motion support, and robust Arabic RTL layout with no horizontal overflow.
